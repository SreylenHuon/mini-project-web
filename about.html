HISTORY

Korean Skin Care

Korean skin care is known for its ability to create glass skin – smooth, baby-soft skin that glows from within. It leads the industry with unique ingredients like rice water, centella asiatica, mugwort, snail mucin, ginseng and fermented ingredients. THE FACE SHOP’s Rice Water Bright Light Cleansing Oil, Dr. Jart+’s Cicapair Tiger Grass Calming Mask and Benton’s Snail Bee Ultimate Serum are just a few examples of Korean skin care products that use inventive natural ingredients. Korean beauty brands are also forerunners in creating and popularizing innovative Korean skin care products, such as cleansing balms like BANILA CO’s Clean It Zero Cleansing Balm, lip masks like LANEIGE’s Lip Sleeping Mask, and sun essences like THANK YOU FARMER’s Sun Project Light Sun Essence.
The game-changing ten-step Korean skin care routine starts with double cleansing using an oil-based cleanser to remove makeup and water-based cleanser to eliminate dirt. Follow with a physical exfoliator or chemical exfoliator to get rid of dead skin cells, a pH-balancing toner to hydrate and prep skin, and then an essence, serum or ampoule for benefits like strengthening the skin barrier. Other steps include a sheet mask – especially one with hyaluronic acid – for extra hydration, eye cream to reduce fine lines, moisturizer to seal in moisture, and sun care for SPF protection.
Take your pick from YesStyle’s wide array of Korean skin care brands like Beauty of Joseon, Nature Republic, TONYMOLY and more! Build beauty routines tailored for your skin type and skin concerns, be it oily skin, dry skin, combination skin, acne-prone skin or sensitized skin.
